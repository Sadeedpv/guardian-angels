| About us

A Sanctuary of Compassion and Care

Founded by Abdu Rasheed Saqafi in 2017, Guardian Angels has been a safe haven for over 100 abandoned elderly individuals—offering love, care, and dignity. What began as a single act of kindness in his own home has grown into a community-driven mission rooted in dignity, humanity, and love. Today, Guardian Angels is more than a shelter — it’s a place of joy, prayer, laughter, healing, and second chances. From medical services to reading corners and cultural programs, we ensure every resident is respected, loved, and remembered.
